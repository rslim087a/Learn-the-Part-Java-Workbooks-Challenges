## **Careful from the** `nextLine()` **trap**
----------------------------------------------

|`nextLine()` Trap | |
| --- | --- |
| Pitfall  | `nextLine()` gets skipped when placed after `nextInt()` , `nextDouble()`, or `nextLong()`. 
| Solution  |Add a throwaway `nextLine()` before the "real" `nextLine()`.
