`>> My name is `**`<name>`**`. I'm `**`<age>`**`  years old, and I'm from  `**`<country>`**`.`

`>> My favorite sport is `**`<sport>`**`. I play for `**`<hours>`**` hour(s) a day`

`>> When I'm tired, I like to play `**`<game>`**`.`
